Fish ID photo automatic reuse

1. Selecting a Fish ID photo automatically links it to the freshness section.
2. The linked image is restored after returning from the identification result page.
3. Separate eye or gill close-ups are optional and override the linked image.
4. Whole-fish images may be rejected if the eye or gills are not clearly visible.
5. Overall freshness remains a model-derived visual score, not a food-safety measurement.
